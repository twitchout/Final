Welcome to the page dedicated to my current favorite games

Spider Man

This is the first iteration in the critically acclaimed trilogy released by Playstation. You follow the story of Peter Parker and help the NYPD defeat his villians. My son and I have both become very enthralled with this game recently.

Crusader Kings III

Play as a King... or a Duke, or even a Baron. In this game you roleplay a medieval house of your choosing. Become encapsulated by the ability to change history by your whims. This game tests your strategy and decision-making skills.

Minecraft

How do you explain Minecraft? It is an open world survival game where every piece of the world is interactive. It is styled after 8 bit cubes. It is a fun and interactive game that you can play with friends or tiny children. My 6 year old son loves this game. He can probably tell you more about this game then me.

Cyberpunk 2077

Dystopian future where corporations run the world. Takes place in Night City the games equivalent to Las Vegas. Follow the story of V as he takes on gangs, corpos, and nomads. Action packed and exciting.
